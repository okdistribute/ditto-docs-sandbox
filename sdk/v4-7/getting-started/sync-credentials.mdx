To get started, go to the Ditto [portal](https://portal.ditto.live/ "portal"):
<Steps>
  <Step >
Create a Ditto account and then log in. (See [Setting Up Account](#setting-up-account))

  </Step>
  <Step >
Set up a new app in the portal. (See [Creating New App](#creating-new-app-once-youve-created-your-ditto-account-and-logged-in))
  </Step>
  <Step >
Obtain your sync credentials for peer-to-peer authentication. (See [Getting Credentials](#getting-credentials))
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Up Account

From the [portal](https://portal.ditto.live/ "portal"):

<Steps>
  <Step >
  Enter your information and password.
  </Step>
  <Step >
  If you want, select **Enroll in multi****‑****factor authentication**.
  </Step>
  <Step >
  Read and select to agree to the [**Terms of Service**](https://www.ditto.live/legal/terms-of-service "Terms of Service").
  </Step>
  <Step >
Complete signup and log in:

- To sign up using Google Single Sign-On, select **Sign up with Google**.
- To sign up using the email entered in the previous step, click **Create Account**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/v4.7/sync-cred-1.webp"/>
</Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating New AppOnce you've created your Ditto account and logged in:

<Steps>
    <Step>
      Click **Create Organization**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Enter the **Name** of your organization and, if available, the **Organization Website URL**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Create Organization**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Let's Create an App!**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Enter a **Name** and a slug for your **App URL**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Select your **App Visibility** preference.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Create App**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/v4.7/sync-cred-2.webp"/>
    </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>
